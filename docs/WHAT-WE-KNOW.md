# WHAT-WE-KNOW

ExamPilot helps Indian students who have a syllabus and exam date but do not know what to study today. The current best user is a serious JEE or NEET dropper, or a college student under exam pressure, who wants a fast daily plan without confusion.

Right now, our goal is not to build a big system. Our goal is to prove three things: students understand the product quickly, students find `Today Plan` useful, and some students are willing to pay `Rs 49` to unlock the full plan. If we prove that, we move to retention next.
